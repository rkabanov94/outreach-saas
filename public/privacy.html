Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025 · outreachminer.com

1. Who We Are

OutreachMiner ("we", "us", "our") operates the website outreachminer.com. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

Account data: When you register, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We do not store payment card details — these are handled entirely by Paddle.

Usage data: We use Simple Analytics, a privacy-first analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not collect personal identifiers. We receive only aggregate, anonymised page view data.

Search data: The websites and email addresses you discover using the Service are processed in your browser session. We do not store them on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate the Service
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To send transactional emails (e.g. receipts, account notifications)
  • To improve the Service based on anonymised analytics

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and account database (EU region)
  • Paddle — payment processing and subscription management
  • Serper — Google Search API (search queries are sent to Serper servers)
  • Simple Analytics — privacy-first, cookie-free analytics

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is removed within 30 days. Aggregated analytics data is retained indefinitely in anonymised form.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to object to or restrict its processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at roman@outreachminer.com.

7. Cookies

We use a single functional cookie to maintain your login session. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. Our analytics provider (Simple Analytics) is fully cookie-free.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and row-level security on our database. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

9. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you by email or via a notice on the Service for material changes.

11. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at roman@outreachminer.com.
