Frequently asked questions

How does the free demo work?
You can search Google for websites in one country with up to 3 keywords — no account required. Upgrading unlocks contact discovery, draft creation, and removes all limits.
What exactly does the tool do?
OutreachMiner searches Google in your target countries and niches, collects relevant websites, scans them for real contact emails, and generates personalised cold email drafts that open directly in Gmail.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
Do you store my emails or contacts?
No. All search results and contacts are processed in your browser session only. We don't store any of the websites or emails you discover.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards via Paddle. Your payment data is handled securely by Paddle — we never see your card details.
